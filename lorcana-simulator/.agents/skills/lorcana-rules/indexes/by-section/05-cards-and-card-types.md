# Section 5: Cards and Card Types

**Comprehensive Rules Version 2.0.1** - Rule References 5.1-5.6

## Overview

This section covers card states, parts of a card, and rules for each card type: Characters, Actions, Items, and Locations.

---

## 5.1 Card States

**Rule refs:** 5.1.1-5.1.8

Cards can have various states during gameplay.

### Ready/Exerted (5.1.1)

- **Ready:** Upright position, can take actions
- **Exerted:** Turned sideways, has acted this turn

### Dry/Drying (5.1.2-5.1.3)

- **Dry:** In play at start of current turn (can quest, challenge, pay costs)
- **Drying:** Played after start of turn (can't quest, challenge, or pay costs until next turn)

### On Top/Under (5.1.4)

- **On top:** Card has other cards stacked beneath it
- **Under:** Card is beneath another card (not in play)

### In a Stack (5.1.5-5.1.7)

- Multiple cards stacked on top of each other
- Only top card is in play
- When stack leaves play, all cards move to same zone
- Common with Shift keyword

### Facedown (5.1.8)

- Cards in inkwell are facedown
- Facedown cards in play zone are private information

## 5.2 Parts of a Card

**Rule refs:** 5.2.1-5.2.12

Each card has identifiable parts:

| Part               | Description                                                        |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Name**           | Card's name (top for actions/items/locations, left for characters) |
| **Version**        | Descriptor differentiating same-name cards                         |
| **Classification** | Category of card (e.g., Hero, Villain, Alien, Prince)              |
| **Ink Cost**       | Number in top left (ink needed to play)                            |
| **Inkwell Symbol** | ○ means card can be inked                                          |
| **Ⓒ (Strength)**   | Damage character deals in challenge                                |
| **⣇ (Willpower)**  | Damage needed to banish                                            |
| **◇ (Lore Value)** | Lore gained from questing or location                              |
| **◊ (Move Cost)**  | Cost to move character to this location                            |
| **Rarity**         | Common, Uncommon, Rare, Super Rare, Legendary                      |
| **Ability Text**   | Special rules for this card                                        |
| **Keyword**        | Bolded ability name (e.g., Rush, Evasive)                          |

## 5.3 Characters

**Rule refs:** 5.3.1-5.3.5

Characters are the primary cards that quest and challenge.

### 5.3.1 Definition

- Have at least one classification
- Have both Ⓒ and ⣇ characteristics
- Put into Play zone when played
- Remain until banished or removed

### 5.3.2 Playing Characters

- Pay ink cost (or alternate cost like Shift)
- Put into play ready or exerted (Bodyguard allows exerted)
- Drying until next turn (can't quest, challenge, or pay costs)

### 5.3.3 Characteristics

- **Ⓒ (Strength):** Damage dealt in challenge
- **⣇ (Willpower):** Damage needed to banish
- **◇ (Lore Value):** Lore gained when questing
- **Classifications:** Categories for other cards to reference

### 5.3.4 Stacking

- Multiple cards with same name can stack via Shift
- Top card is in play
- Damage carries through stack
- All cards move to same zone when stack leaves play

### 5.3.5 Banishment

- When damage ≥ ⣇, character is banished
- Put into owner's discard pile
- Damage removed when leaving play

## 5.4 Actions

**Rule refs:** 5.4.1-5.4.5

Actions are one-time effect cards.

### 5.4.1 Definition

- Give immediate advantage
- Resolved when played, then put in discard

### 5.4.2 Playing Actions

- Pay ink cost
- Put into play zone
- Resolve effect immediately
- Put into discard after effect resolves

### 5.4.3 Songs

Songs are actions with additional cost options:

- Pay ink cost normally, OR
- Use Singer keyword (exert character to sing)
- Use Sing Together keyword (exert multiple characters)

### 5.4.4 Singing (5.4.4.2)

- Exert character with cost ≥ song's ink cost
- Character's cost must equal or exceed song's cost
- "Whenever this character sings" abilities trigger

### 5.4.5 Action Effects

- Can affect any number of targets
- Can create delayed triggered abilities
- Can generate static effects with durations

---

## 5.5 Items

**Rule refs:** 5.5.1-5.5.5

Items provide ongoing effects or activated abilities.

### 5.5.1 Definition

- List "Item" on classification line
- Put into Play zone when played
- Remain until banished or removed

### 5.5.2 Playing Items

- Pay ink cost
- Put into play ready
- Activated abilities can be used same turn played

### 5.5.3 Item Characteristics

- No Ⓒ or ⣇ (can't quest or challenge)
- Can have activated abilities
- Can have static abilities affecting other cards
- Can have costs to banish themselves for effects

### 5.5.4 Banishment

- Items are banished like characters (when removed from play)
- Put into owner's discard pile

### 5.5.5 Card Limits

- Max 4 copies per deck with same full name

---

## 5.6 Locations

**Rule refs:** 5.6.1-5.6.8

Locations provide ongoing benefits and can be challenged.

### 5.6.1 Definition

- List "Location" on classification line
- Put into Play zone when played
- Have move cost and ⣇
- May have ◇ (lore value)

### 5.6.2 Playing Locations

- Pay ink cost
- Put into play
- Can have activated abilities usable same turn

### 5.6.3 Location Characteristics

- **⣇ (Willpower):** Damage needed to banish
- **◊ (Move Cost):** Cost to move character here
- **◇ (Lore Value):** Lore gained at start of turn during Set step

### 5.6.4 Gaining Lore

- During Set step, gain ◇ from each location you control
- Happens before characters can quest

### 5.6.5 Moving Characters

- Pay ◊ to move character to location
- Character doesn't ready or exert
- Multiple characters can occupy same location

### 5.6.6 Challenging Locations

- Can be challenged like characters
- Only challenging character deals damage
- Location deals no damage back

### 5.6.7 Banishment

- When damage ≥ ⣇, location is banished
- Put into owner's discard pile

### 5.6.8 Card Limits

- Can have multiple copies with same name in deck
- Max 4 copies per deck with same full name
