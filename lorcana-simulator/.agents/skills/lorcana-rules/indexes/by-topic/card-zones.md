## All Zones

**Rule refs:** 7.1

Each card exists in exactly one zone at a time.

### Zone List

| Zone        | Public/Private | Description                                   |
| ----------- | -------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Deck**    | Private        | Facedown pile, draw from here                 |
| **Hand**    | Private        | Cards held, played from here                  |
| **Play**    | Public         | Where characters/items/locations are          |
| **Inkwell** | Private        | Ink resources, facedown                       |
| **Discard** | Public         | Banished/discarded cards, faceup              |
| **Bag**     | N/A            | Where triggered abilities wait (non-physical) |

---

## Public vs Private Zones

**Rule refs:** 7.1.2-7.1.3

### Public Zones

**Characteristics:**

- Cards are publicly known
- Can look at cards at any time
- Can count number of cards at any time

**Public Zones:**

- **Play zone:** All cards in play
- **Discard pile:** Faceup, can look at and rearrange yours
- **Bag:** Triggered abilities waiting to resolve

### Private Zones

**Characteristics:**

- Cards aren't publicly known
- Can't look at them unless rule/effect allows
- Can count number of cards at any time
- Revealed portions become public while revealed

**Private Zones:**

- **Deck:** Facedown, can't look at or reorder
- **Hand:** Can look at yours only, not opponents'
- **Inkwell:** Facedown, can't look at any (even yours)

### Facedown Cards in Play Zone

Exception: Facedown cards in Play zone are private

- Can count number of facedown cards
- Can't look at them unless rule/effect allows

## In Play

**Rule refs:** 7.1.5, 7.4

**Key Concept:** Only cards in a player's Play zone are considered "in play."

### What "In Play" Means

- Character's abilities can be used (unless drying)
- Card can be affected by effects
- Card contributes to game state
- Location's ◇ gained during Set step

### What's NOT In Play

- Cards in deck, hand, inkwell, discard
- Cards under another card (in a stack)
- Cards outside the game

### Leaving Play

When card enters any zone from play:

- All gained effects removed
- All damage removed
- All gained characteristics removed
- Card becomes "new card"

## The Bag

**Rule refs:** 7.7

The bag is a non-physical zone for triggered abilities.

### What Goes in Bag

- Only triggered abilities
- Triggered abilities waiting to resolve

### What DOESN'T Go in Bag

- Activated abilities (resolve immediately)
- Resolving actions (resolving immediately)
- Playing characters/items/locations (not triggered abilities)

### Bag Resolution Order

1. Check which players have abilities in bag
2. Active player chooses one of their abilities, resolves it
3. Repeat if active player has more
4. When active player done, pass to next player clockwise
5. Repeat until bag empty

### Key Points

- Abilities added during resolving wait for next check
- Game state check after each effect
- If player leaves game, their abilities in bag cease to exist

## Zone Movement

**Rule refs:** Various (4.2-4.3, 7.2-7.6)

### Common Movements

**From Hand → Play (Playing a Card):**

- Character/Item/Location: Pay cost, put into play
- Action: Pay cost, put into play, resolve effect, then discard

**From Hand → Inkwell (Inking):**

- Put card facedown into inkwell
- Card must have inkwell symbol ○ (unless effect says otherwise)

**From Play → Discard (Banishment):**

- Character/Location with damage ≥ ⣇
- Effects that banish cards
- Actions after resolving

**From Deck → Hand (Drawing):**

- Take top card, put into hand
- Can only draw from own deck

**From Hand → Discard (Discarding):**

- Choose cards from hand, put into discard
- From effects that say "discard"

**Any Zone → Play (Effects):**

- "Play a card from your discard"
- "Put into play" effects
- Follow normal rules for entering play

### Adding Cards to Deck

- If adding "in any order"
- If cards are faceup, order must be known by all
- Cards from stacks can be combined and freely ordered

### Adding Cards to Discard

- If multiple cards enter discard at same time
- Owner chooses order

## Specific Zone Rules

### Deck (7.2)

- Private, facedown, single pile
- Can't look at or reorder during game
- Can count remaining cards

### Hand (7.3)

- Private, look at yours only
- No maximum hand size
- Minimum: 0 cards
- Can rearrange however you like

### Play (7.4)

- Public zone
- Can look at any player's cards in play
- Can rearrange your Play zone if clear to all
- Facedown cards are private

### Inkwell (7.5)

- Private, facedown
- Can't look at cards (even yours)
- Can count number of cards
- No limit to number of cards

### Discard (7.6)

- Public, faceup
- Can look at any player's discard
- Can rearrange your own discard
- Multiple cards entering: owner chooses order

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I look at my opponent's hand?**
A: No. Hand is a private zone. You can count how many cards they have, but can't look at them.

**Q: Can I rearrange my discard pile?**
A: Yes. You can look at and rearrange your own discard at any time.

**Q: Are cards in my inkwell considered "in play"?**
A: No. Only cards in the Play zone are "in play." Inkwell cards are ink resources only.

**Q: What happens to a card's damage when it leaves play?**
A: Damage is removed. When a card leaves play, all damage is removed and it becomes a "new card."

**Q: Can I look at cards in my deck?**
A: No. Deck is private and facedown. Can't look at or reorder during game.

**Q: What happens when a card goes from play to the bag?**
A: Nothing goes to the bag except triggered abilities. Cards don't go to the bag - it's not a physical zone.
