# Turn Actions - By Topic Index

**Comprehensive Rules Version 2.0.1** - Effective February 5, 2026

## Overview

This topic covers all actions available during your Main Phase: Inking a card, Playing a card, Using an activated ability, Questing, Challenging, and Moving a character to a location.

---

## Quick Questions

| Question                              | Answer Location             |
| ------------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **What can I do during my turn?**     | See Available Actions below |
| **How many times can I act?**         | No limit (as long as legal) |
| **What order do actions happen?**     | Any order you choose        |
| **Can I act during opponent's turn?** | Generally no (see below)    |

---

## Available Actions

**Rule refs:** 3.3, 4.1-4.7

During your Main Phase, you may take any of these turn actions in any order:

| Action                    | Description                              | Frequency                                 |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| **Ink a card**            | Put card from hand into inkwell facedown | 1 per turn (unless effect says otherwise) |
| **Play a card**           | Pay cost to put card into play           | No limit (if can pay cost)                |
| **Use activated ability** | Pay cost to use ability                  | No limit (if can pay cost)                |
| **Quest**                 | Exert character to gain lore             | No limit (if character can quest)         |
| **Challenge**             | Exert character to fight                 | No limit (if character can challenge)     |
| **Move character**        | Pay move cost to move to location        | No limit (if can pay cost)                |

### No Limit to Actions

- Can take any number of turn actions
- Must complete one action before starting next
- Can take same action multiple times (if legal)

---

## Ink a Card

**Rule refs:** 4.2

Put a card from hand into inkwell as ink resource.

### Requirements

- Can ink only 1 card per turn (unless effect)
- Card must have inkwell symbol ○
- Can't ink during opponent's turn

### Process

1. Choose card from hand with ○
2. Put that card into inkwell facedown and ready

### Effects from Other Cards

- Some cards allow adding "additional" cards to inkwell
- Announce effect, then follow normal inking process

---

## Play a Card

**Rule refs:** 4.3

Pay ink cost (or alternate cost) to put card into play.

### Requirements

- Must be during your turn (unless card says otherwise)
- Card must be in your hand
- Must be able to pay cost

### Process

1. Reveal card from hand
2. Choose zone (for characters)
3. Pay cost (ink or alternate)
4. Card is "played"

### Card Types

**Characters/Items/Locations:**

- Enter play zone
- Remain until banished

**Actions:**

- Enter play zone
- Effect resolves immediately
- Put into discard after effect

### Playing "For Free"

- Some effects allow playing "for free"
- Don't pay ink cost
- Must follow all other rules

### Songs

Actions with additional cost options:

- Pay ink cost normally, OR
- Exert character to sing (Singer, Sing Together keywords)

## Use an Activated Ability

**Rule refs:** 4.4

Pay cost to use an ability on a card.

### Requirements

- Must be during your turn
- Character must be dry (for abilities with ○ cost)
- Character can't be questing or in challenge
- Must be able to pay all costs

### Costs

May include:

- ○ (exerting the card)
- Paying ink
- Other specified costs

### "For Free" Abilities

- "Use for free" = no cost except ○
- ○ must still be paid if required

### Same Turn Played

- **With ○:** Character must be dry (can't use same turn)
- **Without ○:** Can use same turn character enters play

---

## Quest

**Rule refs:** 4.5

Exert a character to gain lore.

### Requirements

- Character must be dry
- Character can't be exerted
- Character can't be drying (played after start of turn)

### Process

1. Choose dry character
2. Exert that character
3. Gain lore equal to character's ◇ value

### Drying Characters

Can't quest:

- Just played this turn
- Didn't start turn in play

### Negative Lore

If character has negative ◇:

- Gain 0 lore from questing
- Character's ◇ value unchanged (still negative)

---

## Challenge

**Rule refs:** 4.6

Exert your character to fight opponent's character or location.

### Requirements

**Challenging Character:**

- Must be dry
- Must not be exerted
- Must be able to choose target

**Challenged Character:**

- Must be exerted (opponent's character)
- OR must be a location

### Restrictions

Keywords that affect challenging:

- **Evasive:** Can't be challenged except by Evasive
- **Bodyguard:** Must challenge if able
- **Ward:** Can't be chosen
- **Alert:** Ignores Evasive

### Process

1. Choose your dry character
2. Choose opponent's exerted character OR location
3. Challenge Damage Step: Each deals Ⓒ to other simultaneously

### Keywords

- **Challenger +N:** +N Ⓒ while challenging
- **Rush:** Can challenge while drying

---

## Move a Character

**Rule refs:** 4.7

Pay move cost to move character to location you control.

### Requirements

- Must control character
- Must control destination location
- Must be able to pay move cost

### Process

1. Choose character you control
2. Choose location you control
3. Pay move cost ◊
4. Character now at that location

### Character State

- Character doesn't ready or exert
- Can be ready or exerted when moving
- Multiple characters can be at same location

## Timing and Restrictions

### During Your Turn Only

Most actions require it to be your turn:

- Ink a card
- Play a card (unless card says otherwise)
- Use activated ability
- Quest
- Challenge

### Action Requirements Summary

| Action        | Key Requirement                         | What Prevents It                                |
| ------------- | --------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Ink**       | Card must have ○                        | Already inked this turn                         |
| **Play**      | During your turn, pay cost              | Can't pay cost                                  |
| **Activate**  | Character dry (for ○), not in challenge | Character exerted, questing, or challenging     |
| **Quest**     | Character dry, not exerted, not drying  | Character exerted or drying                     |
| **Challenge** | Character dry, valid target exists      | Character exerted or drying, or no valid target |
| **Move**      | Pay move cost, control both             | Can't pay cost                                  |

### Acting During Opponent's Turn

Generally can't act during opponent's turn EXCEPT:

- Triggered abilities resolving from bag
- Effects that specifically allow it
- Abilities that say "during any turn"

## Quick Reference

**Main Phase Checklist:**

- [ ] Ready cards to act
- [ ] Have ink to pay costs
- [ ] Have valid targets
- [ ] Check all requirements met

**Action Flow:**

1. Choose action
2. Check requirements
3. Pay costs
4. Resolve action
5. Handle resulting triggers
6. Repeat or choose different action

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I play multiple characters in one turn?**
A: Yes, as long as you can pay their costs.

**Q: Can I quest and challenge with same character?**
A: No. Both require exerting the character, and exerted characters can't act again this turn.

**Q: Can I use activated abilities during opponent's turn?**
A: Generally no. Activated abilities can only be used during your turn.

**Q: Do I have to do all actions each turn?**
A: No. You can do any number (including zero) of each action type.

**Q: What order should I do my actions in?**
A: Any order you choose. Plan based on what you want to accomplish.

**Q: Can I ink a card the turn I play it?**
A: No, you can't ink from hand. Cards must already be in hand to ink.
