# Keywords Quick Reference - By Topic Index

**Comprehensive Rules Version 2.0.1** - Effective February 5, 2026

## Overview

This topic provides a quick reference for all keyword abilities in Disney Lorcana. Keywords are abilities with short, bolded names that provide specific effects.

---

## Challenge Keywords

### Evasive (8.6)

**"This [Type] can't be challenged except by a character with Evasive."**

- Creates targeting restriction
- Normal characters can't challenge Evasive
- Alert ignores this restriction

### Alert (8.2)

**"This character ignores the challenging restriction of Evasive."**

- Allows challenging Evasive characters
- Doesn't gain Evasive

### Bodyguard (8.3)

**Two abilities:**

1. "This character may enter play exerted rather than ready."
2. "If an opponent would choose one of your characters to challenge, they must choose this character or another character with Bodyguard if able."

### Challenger +N (8.5)

**"While this character is challenging, they gain +N Ⓒ."**

- Stacks with other +N effects
- Only applies while challenging

### Rush (8.9)

**"This character can challenge as though they were in play at the beginning of your turn."**

- Allows challenging while drying
- Character still drying (can't quest, pay costs)

---

## Damage Keywords

### Resist +N (8.8)

**"Damage that would be dealt to this character or location is reduced by N."**

- Reduces damage dealt
- Stacks (+N ability)
- Doesn't affect damage counters put onto card

---

## Quest Keywords

### Support (8.13)

**"Whenever this character quests, you may add this character's Ⓒ to another chosen character's Ⓒ this turn."**

- Triggered ability
- Optional when questing
- Adds Ⓒ to another character for rest of turn

### Reckless (8.7)

**Two abilities:**

1. "This character can't quest."
2. "You can't declare the end of your turn if this character is ready and can challenge an opposing character or location."

---

## Alternate Cost Keywords

### Shift (8.10)

**"If you have a character in play with the same name as this card, you may play this character by paying their Shift cost instead of their ink cost. If you do, put this card on top of another character you have in play with the same name."**

- Static ability (alternate cost)
- Must have same-name character in play
- Put shifted card on top of same-name character
- Variants: [Classification] Shift, Universal Shift

### Singer N (8.11)

**"This character can pay the alternate cost of a song as though they had cost N instead of their normal ink cost."**

- Allows singing by exerting character
- Character counts as cost N for singing

### Sing Together N (8.12)

**"Instead of paying the ink cost of this card, you can exert a number of your or your teammates' characters with total ink cost N or greater to play this card without paying its ink cost."**

- Can use your OR teammates' characters
- Combine costs of multiple characters

---

## Protection Keywords

### Ward (8.15)

**"Your opponents can't choose this card when resolving an effect."**

- Creates targeting restriction
- Doesn't prevent non-targeted effects

### Vanish (8.14)

**"When this character is chosen by an opponent as part of resolving an action's effect, banish this character."**

- Triggered ability
- Resolves after action's effect
- If character moved zones before resolves, does nothing

---

## Other Keywords

### Boost +N (8.4)

**"Once during your turn, you may pay N to put top card of your deck facedown under this card."**

- Activated ability
- Can use once per turn per source
- Facedown card can't be looked at
- Cards under aren't in play

---

## Stacking Rules

**Keywords with +N:**

- Stack with other effects that provide +N
- Multiple instances combine their values
- Examples: Challenger +N, Resist +N, Boost +N

**Keywords without +N:**

- Don't stack with themselves
- If card has keyword and gains it again, only first applies
- Examples: Evasive, Rush, Ward, Alert, Support

## Keyword Combinations

**Common Synergies:**

| Keywords                 | Interaction                                                          |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Alert + Evasive**      | Alert ignores Evasive, can challenge Evasive characters              |
| **Bodyguard + Ward**     | Bodyguard must be challenged if able, but Ward prevents being chosen |
| **Challenger + Resist**  | Modified Ⓒ still reduced by Resist                                   |
| **Support + Challenger** | Support boosts Ⓒ for challenges too                                  |
| **Rush + drying**        | Rush allows challenging while drying                                 |
