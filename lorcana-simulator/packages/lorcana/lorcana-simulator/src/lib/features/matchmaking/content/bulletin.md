Over the past few days, I have focused extensively on fixing the core mechanical issues reported during the alpha. While there are still a handful of mechanics not functioning exactly as intended, the vast majority are fixed, and I am confident the remaining ones will be resolved very soon.

## April 7, 2026

### 🚀 Lorcanito v2 is here — rebuilt from the ground up. New features, open source, and community prizes incoming.

Hey everyone! After months of work, I'm proud to announce that Lorcanito has been completely rewritten from scratch.

**Why a rewrite?**

Lorcanito evolved into something very different from what it was originally designed to be — thanks to all of your feedback and input. But that also meant the underlying architecture was holding us back. Every small change had become risky and complicated, which is why updates slowed down and quality suffered. That era is over.

**What this means for the community**

First, I can now safely implement the features you've been asking for. The new architecture means faster, better updates across the board. Second, my running costs are dropping significantly — and I'm funneling that money into community prizes. Third, Lorcanito is now open source. You can contribute directly to improving the platform, and if I'm ever unable to continue, someone else can pick up where I left off.

**What's new right now**

- 🎬 Match replays — rewatch your games
- 🤖 Play against bots — better solo playtesting
- 🔜 Coming soon: custom board states so you can practice specific lines and goldfish your deck

**What's temporarily missing**

The new platform may not yet have every feature the current one does. I'll be adding them back incrementally to make sure each one is an improvement. Best-of-three and ranked are intentionally disabled for now — not because they aren't ready, but because I want the server and UI to be rock solid before competitive play goes live. Exploitable glitches in ranked would be a problem for everyone.

**🙏 Help wanted**

We're about to go live and I need your help with playtesting and stress testing. I've been catching small issues on my own, but it'll go much faster with community support. Here's how you can help:

- → Try the new UI and matchmaking whenever you can
- → Report bugs and share feedback — I'll be addressing them as fast as possible

**📅 Timeline**

We're less than a month from the new set. Here's the plan: the current season continues on the current platform as-is. I'll gradually move non-competitive queues to the new platform. The pre-release event will run on the new platform only. After the new set launches, the old platform will be retired.

Thanks for sticking with Lorcanito — let's make v2 great together. 💪
